About Deepseas Taekwondo Club

Deepseas taekwondo was formed in 2022 by master Issac Mutuku a black belt holder Deepseas Taekwondo Club is dedicated to teaching the art of Taekwondo to students of all ages and skill levels.

  • Experienced instructors committed to your growth and development.
  • A supportive community focused on discipline and respect.
  • Comprehensive training programs tailored to meet individual needs.

Our mission is to promote physical fitness, mental strength, and personal development through the practice of Taekwondo. Join us to embark on a journey of self-improvement and empowerment.

Expert Instructors

Learn from highly skilled and experienced Taekwondo masters dedicated to your progress.

Community Spirit

Join a supportive and welcoming community that fosters respect, discipline, and camaraderie.

Regular Events

Participate in regular competitions, seminars, and events to test and showcase your skills.
